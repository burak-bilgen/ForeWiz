# ForeWiz — App Store Description

## App Name
ForeWiz — Personal Weather Assistant

## Subtitle (iOS 15+)
Your weather sidekick.

## Promotional Text (Optional)
Smart outfit suggestions, activity timing, and AI-powered recommendations.

## Description

**ForeWiz answers your daily weather questions before you even ask them.**

No dashboards. No clutter. Just clear answers: "Should I go outside?", "What should I wear?", "When's the best time for a walk?"

### AI-Powered Features

- **AI Outfit Tips** — Get smart clothing recommendations based on current weather conditions. Powered on-device with natural language processing.
- **Smart Notifications** — Severe weather alerts, best time recommendations, and daily summaries delivered right when you need them.
- **Temperature Trend Chart** — Interactive bezier curve chart showing hourly comfort scores for the next 12 hours.

### All Features

- **Personalized Comfort** — Learn your temperature sensitivity and activity preferences during onboarding.
- **Outdoor Score (0-100)** — Every weather factor synthesized into one clear score.
- **Best Activity Windows** — Know exactly when conditions are ideal for running, walking, or cycling.
- **Severe Weather Alerts** — Get notified about heat waves, storms, high UV, and more.
- **Multi-Location Support** — Save and switch between cities. Beautiful map-based search.
- **Widget** — Home screen and Lock Screen widgets with gradient backgrounds.
- **Today Extension** — Quick glance at current conditions.
- **Siri Shortcuts** — "Hey Siri, what's the weather?" integrations.
- **Dynamic Design** — Animated backgrounds that change with weather conditions.
- **Glassmorphism UI** — Beautiful translucent cards with spring animations.
- **Dark Mode** — Designed for dark-first experience.
- **Bilingual** — Full English and Turkish support.
- **Accessibility** — VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion support.
- **Background Refresh** — Always up-to-date weather data.
- **Privacy** — All data stays on your device. No tracking.

### Premium Features

- Extended 14-day forecast
- Full weather analytics and insights
- Priority time-sensitive notifications
- Ad-free experience

### Privacy

- Location is used only for local weather — no background tracking.
- All preferences stored on-device via SwiftData.
- No analytics or third-party SDKs.
- No data leaves your device.
